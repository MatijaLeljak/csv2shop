What do we offer?

CSV2SHOP is a handy tool for online stores that allows you to quickly add product features and images to your site.

Product features

Import to your site detailed product specifications that will help buyers make their choice when buying.

Product images

Import high-quality product images to your site that will help buyers consider a potential purchase from all angles.

How it works?

Find a product on our website.

Download file for import.

Use the standard product import functionality of your site.

Done! Product features and images are added to your site.

Supported CMS?

From our catalog, you can import information about products to sites created on the most popular CMS. It is also possible to prepare an import file in your unique format.

Wordpress
Magento
OpenCart

What are the advantages using the CSV2SHOP?

Using the CSV2SHOP service provides a number of advantages. The main advantages are:

Save time

Save time on searching for information and images. Spend 2-3 minutes vs 30-60 minutes per one product.

Less errors

There will be less errors on your site, because information will not be entered manually, but will be imported from the downloaded files.

Better content

Now you have more time to prepare unique text or SEO product descriptions.

More purchases

More purchases, because there is complete information about the product to make a purchase decision.

Products in the catalog

Use the search on the site or the list of sections to find the desired product in our catalog and import it to your site.

Didn't find a product in the catalog?

Send us a request, and within 1 hour we will add the product to our catalog so that you can import it to your site.
